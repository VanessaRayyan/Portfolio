Quiz App

This is a quiz that I created using JavaScript and JQuery. Its purpose is to test your knowledge on countries and which continent it belongs to. You will get 4 options to choose from. It also keeps track of what question you are on and what your score is. At every submit, you will be notified whether the answer is correct or not. At the end of the quiz, you will be shown your score and have the option to restart it.
